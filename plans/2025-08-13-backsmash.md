# Strength
*We broke our back trying to carry a cow on our back.*
- An Unknown Dumbass

## What to train?
Prioritize back and rehab. Plan rehab for the following:
- Lower back
- Knee and hip
- Left elbow
- Rotator cuff
- Serratus anterior

## How to train?
Train close to failure. Try to increase resistance for all exercises.

## The plan
The rehab is not clearly planned out. Use judgment.

- Upper 1
	- barbell row
	- db overhead press / overhead tricep extension
	- db pullover / db lateral raise
	- pullup / dip
	- back extension

- Lower 1
	- seated good morning / hip abduction-adduction
	- squat
	- romanian deadlift / bulgarian split squat
	- back extension / candlestick

- Upper 2
