# Rehab

*Rehab is the only thing fighting the constant functional decay of the body.*
- An Unknown Dumbass

## Grievances

Rehab is tough on a mere mortal's psyche.
It does not provide the satisfaction of strength or cardio training.
There is no fancy end result.
It is just a constant grind to retain bare minimum functionality.
That bare minimum, however, is assumed in all other aspects of training.
Now, here I am, wasting my time and energy to simply diagnose the weak points.
Is my elbow clicking a triceps or a tendon issue?
Is my left shoulder missing a particular muscle?
What is that big dimple below my shoulder blade?
Before I can diagnose and fix even one problem, many others pop up with malice.

## Way forward

There is no alternative to detailed tracking
when you do not get any subconscious feedbacks.
A human's working memory alone is not enough to deal with rehab work.
Luckily, this repo already exists and provides a way to unload most of the load.
First, an honest attempt will be made to diagnose specific issues.
Second, research and experimentation will lead to precise rehab plans.
Third, goals should be explicitly mentioned to avoid analysis paralysis.
Frequent test-retest should accompany the rigorous plans as well.
